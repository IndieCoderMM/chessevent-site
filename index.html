"Hello! Chess World" Global Chess Championship 2022

The Global Chess Championship 2022 is a series of three chess tournaments and the final qualifier for the Candidates Tournament. The top two finishers will qualify for the Candidates Tournament 2022, which is the final qualification stage for the Global Chess Championship 2023. It's organized by World Chess. 24 of the world's top players take part in the Series.

2022.11.21(Mon) ~ 25(Fri)

@ International Chess Center, Paris, France

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to the chess lessons taught by the most-experienced international grandmasters.

Tournaments

Watch the exciting over-the-board chess matches by the top players and learn from them.

Practice

Play chess with other players over a chessboard, rather than playing online.

Theory

Study the important theory about the openings, middle game and endgame.

Community

Become part of the amazing Chess community to discuss, study and play.
